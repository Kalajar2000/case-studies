How I work

  1. Map awareness and sophistication. Where the market already is decides whether the ad opens with a problem, a mechanism, or a claim. Skipping this is why most creative tests are unreadable.
  2. Write hooks per angle before anything is designed. The angle is the unit of testing, not the asset. Design comes after the message is chosen.
  3. Run statics and UGC video in parallel. Same angles, two formats, so the winning message is separable from the winning format.
  4. Brief editors and creators so they execute once. A brief that needs three rounds of clarification is a brief that was not finished.
  5. Iterate on hook rate, hold rate, CTR and CPC using 3:2:2 testing, and kill on evidence rather than on taste.
What I don't do: I don't edit video and I don't buy media. I hand finished briefs, hooks and scripts to your editors, and the numbers back to whoever runs the account. If you need one person to do all three, I'm the wrong hire and I'd rather say so now.

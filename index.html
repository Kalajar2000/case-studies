How I work

  1. Map awareness and sophistication. Where the market already sits decides whether the ad opens with a problem, a mechanism, or a claim. Skipping this step is the single most common reason a creative test produces unreadable results.
  2. Write hooks per angle before anything is designed. The angle is the unit of testing, not the asset. Design follows the message, never the reverse.
  3. Run statics and UGC video in parallel. Same angles, two formats. This separates the winning message from the winning format, which a single-format test cannot do.
  4. Brief editors and creators so they execute once. A brief that needs three rounds of clarification was not finished when it was sent. Briefs go out with the hook, the beat structure, the references and the reason.
  5. Iterate on hook rate, hold rate, CTR and CPC using 3:2:2 hook/body/CTA testing, and kill on evidence rather than on taste.
What I don't do: I don't edit video and I don't buy media. I hand finished briefs, hooks and scripts to your editors, and the numbers back to whoever runs the account. If you need one person to do all three, I'm the wrong hire and I'd rather say so now.
